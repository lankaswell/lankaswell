🏄 Mirissa Point

Mirissa Point is one of the most popular surf breaks on Sri Lanka's south coast. It offers long right-hand rides and generally mellow conditions compared to some nearby reef breaks.

🌊 Reef + Beach Break
🏄 Beginner → Intermediate
📅 Best Season: Nov–Apr
🌡️ Warm Water Year Round
📈 Best Tide: Mid Tide
↗ Main Direction: Right-Hander

Wave Characteristics

Mostly right-hand waves peeling over a reef with some softer inside sections. Good place for progressing surfers and fun long rides when the swell is clean.

Hazards

Shallow reef at low tide, crowds during peak season, and occasional strong currents during larger swells.

Best Conditions

Clean morning winds, mid tide and south-west swells generally provide the best surf.
