Keep LankaSwell alive 🌊

I’m building LankaSwell completely on my own — somewhere between coding sessions, surf checks, and long rides across Sri Lanka. This is not a company with funding or a team behind it. It’s just me trying to turn an idea into something useful for surfers here.

I want to keep this platform free for everyone — no subscriptions, no paywalls, no hidden tricks. But running surf cameras, hosting data, and keeping everything alive does have real costs. If you like the project and want to support it, even a small coffee helps keep it running and growing 🌊
