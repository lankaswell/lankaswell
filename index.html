THE LANKASWELL PROJECT

Hey there!

Watch free live surf cameras across Sri Lanka, discover new breaks, and see what's happening in the lineup in real time.
LankaSwell is an independent, self-funded project building free live surf cameras across Sri Lanka. No company, no investors, just a simple idea: make the ocean more visible before you paddle out. These are the spots covered by LankaSwell so far!


LankaSwell is free to use and aims to remain that way. Donations simply help keep the cameras online and allow new spots to be added along the coast.

Do you want to participate or collaborate to LankaSwell? See how below!

Curious about the philosophy behind the project, privacy, or how LankaSwell works? Read the Principles page.

LIVE SURF CAMERAS

Our surf cameras are live daily from sunrise to sunset (6AM–6PM).
