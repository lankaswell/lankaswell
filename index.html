KEEP LANKASWELL ALIVE

Hey there! I am Alex, nice to meet you!

LankaSwell is my independent project to make surf conditions more visible and accessible in real time. It is self-funded and built step by step, with the goal of creating a free network of surf cams that help surfers, travelers, and locals understand the ocean before they enter it.

If you like what I am building or you find it useful before a surf session, even a small coffee makes a real difference. It directly supports hosting, development, and expanding surf camera coverage along the coast of Sri Lanka ☕

BE PART OF LANKASWELL

Some of our streaming nodes run on repurposed devices, giving a second life to hardware that would otherwise go unused. Lankaswell is built step by step, with the goal of creating a free and open network of surf cams across Sri Lanka.

Do you have a great ocean view? Run a surf school, cafe, or beachfront place? You might already be in the perfect spot to host a camera. We are always open to collaborations, partnerships, and new ideas.
