KEEP LANKASWELL ALIVE

Hey there! I’m Alex, nice to meet you.

LankaSwell is my independent project to make surf conditions in Sri Lanka more visible and accessible in real time. The goal is to create a free network of surf cams that helps surfers, travelers, and locals understand the ocean before they enter it.

Everything is self-funded and built step by step. If you enjoy the project or find it useful before a surf session, even a small coffee genuinely helps support hosting, development, and new cameras along the coast ☕

BE PART OF LANKASWELL

Some LankaSwell streaming nodes run on repurposed devices, giving a second life to hardware that would otherwise go unused. The network is built slowly and independently, one camera at a time.

Do you run a surf school, beachfront café, guesthouse, or have an ocean view? You might already be in the perfect spot to host a camera.
