🏄 Marshmallow

Marshmallow is a fun and forgiving beach break located on Sri Lanka's south coast. Known for its softer waves and sandy bottom, it is a favorite spot for beginners, surf lessons and surfers looking for playful conditions. This live surf cam helps you check wave size, crowd levels and weather conditions before heading out.

🌊 Beach Break
🏄 Beginner Friendly
📅 Best Season: Nov–Apr
🌡️ Warm Water Year Round
📈 Best Tide: Mid Tide
↔ Lefts & Rights

Wave Characteristics

Soft, rolling waves breaking over sand. Marshmallow offers a mellow takeoff zone and forgiving conditions that make it ideal for learning, improving fundamentals and enjoying relaxed surf sessions. On bigger swells it can provide longer rides and fun walls for intermediate surfers.

Hazards

Generally one of the safer breaks in the area thanks to its sandy bottom. The main hazards are crowds during peak season, beginner traffic in the lineup and occasional currents when larger swells are running.

Best Conditions

Clean morning winds and small-to-medium south-west swells usually provide the best conditions. Marshmallow works across a wide range of tides but is often at its most user-friendly around mid tide.
